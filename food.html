Food In Ireland

They Are some of my favorite Irish Foods!

Traditional Irish Breakfast

A "Full Irish" breakfast consisit of sausages ,rashers(bacons),eggs,black pudding,white pudding and toast

Often there will be a grilled tomato as well as mushrooms,and baked beans

And of course,no breakfast is complete without a lovely pot of tea

Banger Of Mash

The clsic of sausages,mashed potato and gravy is not unique to Ireland ,but what makes it special is the Irish Sausages. Most Countries have their own way of making sausages,and they are one thing I miss from home if I'm away travelling !
